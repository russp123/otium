Frequently Asked Questions

What is Otium Elegentia? +
Otium Elegentia is a gallery that celebrates student life, creativity, and personal growth through various collections that showcase hobbies, dreams, and experiences.
How can I participate in the gallery? +
Students interested in sharing their passions can join the gallery by contacting us through our "Get in Touch" section or connecting with us on social media.
What types of collections are showcased? +
The gallery features a wide range of collections, including art, music, sports, literature, and travel, reflecting the diverse interests and talents of students.
Where is Otium Elegentia located? +
Otium Elegentia is located at Pampanga State University, Sto. Tomas Campus.
How can I contact Otium Elegentia? +
You can reach us via email at otiumelegentia@mail.co or connect with us through our social media channels.
